About Japan Tours

Your trusted partner in discovering the beauty of Japan

Our Story

Japan Tours was founded with a passion for sharing the incredible beauty, rich culture, and unique experiences that Japan has to offer. We are a team of travel enthusiasts who have spent years exploring every corner of this amazing country.

From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene bamboo forests of Arashiyama, from the iconic Mount Fuji to the peaceful temples of Kyoto, we curate authentic experiences that showcase the best of Japan.

Our mission is to provide travelers with unforgettable journeys that go beyond typical tourist attractions, offering deep cultural immersion and meaningful connections with local communities.

Expert Guides

Our local guides are passionate experts who provide authentic insights into Japanese culture, history, and traditions.

Authentic Experiences

We focus on genuine cultural experiences that connect you with the real Japan, not just tourist attractions.

Small Groups

Our tours are designed for small groups to ensure personalized attention and a more intimate experience.
